If you are having suicidal thoughts, reach out to your friends and family, tell them your feelings and problems, ask them to help you. But if you think they are inapproachable, then explore online suicide websites. There are many websites that priotize in helping people with suicide. You can also find professional help such as counselors and psychiatrists. You can also call Crisis Hotlines such as the National Center for Mental Health (NCMH) where you can call them via landline: Dial 1553 (toll-free), and mobile: 0917-899-8727 (Globe). Another Crisis Hotline is HOPELINE Philippines where you can call them via landline: (02) 8804-4673, and mobile: 0917-558-4673 (Globe) 0918-878-4673 (Smart). This proves that you are not alone in fighting suicide.
